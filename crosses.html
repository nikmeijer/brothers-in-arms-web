    Victoria Cross

    The Victoria Cross is the premier Operational Gallantry award given for "most conspicuous bravery, or some daring or pre-eminent act of valour or self sacrifice, or extreme devotion to duty in the prsence of the enemy".

    George Cross

    The George Cross is the premier award given for non-operational gallantry or gallantry not in the presence of an enemy.
